Why Valentine's Day is my most favorite time of the year and the best holiday ever!
Once February arrives in Michigan, winter has been dragging on - even if you are a snow and cold enthusiast. Then along comes February 14! Valentine's Day!
Romance fills the air.
People's reactions vary from thoughts of bah-humbug, it's just a Hallmark holiday to thoughts of love or the possibility of a new lover.

So whether you choose to ignore Valentine's Day, or celebrate with a pet, friend, significant other or find a new love, remember that in a little over a month, it's St. Patrick's day and the beer is green!
